The Story...

Bytes and Bits started with an idea from its founder Tasha Penwell. As she started her role as an educator at Hocking College she became aware of two things that many of her students didn't always have access to that would be vital for their success as a college student and their future professions:

  • Access to snacks to keep the distractions from being hungry at bay
  • Understanding of how vital professional development reading is for their growth

Bytes and Bits

The goal of Bytes and Bits is to help support community learning and "break the cycle of poverty" that many find themselves in when access and opportunities to education is limited or hard to learn from. Our goal is to provide the following:

  • Access to learning resources that are easy to follow Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • Access to learning resources to learn new coding skills
